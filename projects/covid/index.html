Our Covid-19 Detection System

Covid-19 Chest X-Ray Detection System

Our purpose it to provide a testing solution to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic. We want to provide quick and accurate detection of Covid through lung x-rays to areas that lack the available medical personale to test their surronding population.

Our Application

Our Automated Covid-19 Detection System is a website application that we have made available to all through a url (given below).

Our application can process images of chest rays from multiple different patients instantaneously to determine if the patient is healthy, has pneumonia, or has covid infected lungs.

This application is a spin-off of our team's Automated Malaria Early Detection System.

Motivation

Our team is motivated to make an impact on the world and its current experience with the global pandemic with our application where Covid-19 can be quickly diagnosed and more accessible. With this application, we intend to reduce the time overhead with Covid diagnosis and therefore give back time to the medical professionals
